Disability Research

I was an invited panel member at the Inclusion Summit hosted by Emory University. I spoke about my personal experience as a college student with a disability, as well as my research on disability legislation. Prior to the Summit I used computational linguistics to conduct research on the ways that Federal Legislation portrayed individuals with disabilities over time. From this experience I learned about new and unique research tools and how to apply them to information I gathered and further improved my presentation skills.
