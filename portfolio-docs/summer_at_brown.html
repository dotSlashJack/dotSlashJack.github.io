Summer@Brown Instructor

I designed and taught a new course, for the Brown University Summer@Brown program. The course entitled From Idea to Publication: Building Your Own Research Project (CEPC0911), stepped students through the basics of research, including systematic reviews and citations, basic statistics and hypothesis testing, data visualization and presentation, academic writing standards with a focus on evidence-based research methods, and evidence synthesis techniques. I taught the class online for six weeks and supervised a TA. The class size was 20 students ranging from high school to first year of college.
