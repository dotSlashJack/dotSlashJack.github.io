She fell in love with
The eyes that changed their color
depending on the clothes she was wearing.
The hair that she cut herself
and that shone a shade of red more beautiful than any autumn leaves.
The voice that nobody could forget,
the one that said said "good morning" in the loveliest way.
The hands with the most elegant painted nails
that were waiting to be gently held as a reminder that everything was ok.

She fell in love with
The quiet girl
who knew way more than she ever let on.
The busiest and most hard working person
who still made time every day to encourage those who needed it the most.
The sarcastic comments and "difficult" attitude
that made for the best tweets.
The heart that had been hurt over and over
but deserved a love greater than anything a person could ever provide.

She fell in love with
herself.
Or at least she will some day
