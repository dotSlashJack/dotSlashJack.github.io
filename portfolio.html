Undergraduate Thesis: "A Verb-Based Approach to Mining Adverse Drug Side-Effects from Online Health Care Forums"

My undergraduate thesis used methods from computational linguistics to parse health care forums and automatically extract mentions of adverse drug side-effects. The software pipline I created used Stanford CoreNLP, a custom support vector machine (SVM), a custom data parser, and other tools written in python and R. I was able to identify several side-effects that were mentioned on the drug insert, in the FDA FEARS database, and a couple of previously unidentified side-effects.
